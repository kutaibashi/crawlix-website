## Overview

Select fonts for your exported PDF reports to match your agency's brand guidelines.

**Requires Agency tier.**

## Setting Fonts

Go to **Settings > White Label > Typography**.

## Font Fields

### Header Font

Applied to heading elements (H1, H2, H3) in PDF reports. Choose a font that establishes the visual hierarchy and personality of your reports.

Options include a curated selection of professional fonts — both serif and sans-serif.

### Body Font

Applied to body text, table content, captions, and supporting copy in PDF reports.

For readability in dense reports, a clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Open Sans, Source Sans) or a traditional serif (e.g., Georgia, Merriweather) works well.

## Available Fonts

Crawlix provides a curated list of professional fonts covering:

- **Geometric sans-serif** — clean, modern feel
- **Humanist sans-serif** — friendly and highly readable
- **Transitional serif** — professional and editorial
- **Slab serif** — bold and distinctive

The available fonts are embedded in PDF exports — no web font loading required.

## Preview

Click **Preview** to see a sample report page rendered with your selected fonts before exporting.

## Combining Fonts

A common and effective approach:

- **Header font**: a more distinctive or branded font
- **Body font**: a highly legible, neutral font

Avoid combining two very similar fonts — the difference won't be noticeable and doesn't add value.
