## Overview

Agency tier users can replace Crawlix branding in exported reports with their own company identity. This makes PDF, HTML, and other exported reports appear as your own agency's deliverables.

**Requires Agency tier. White-labeling applies to exported reports only — it does not change the Crawlix desktop app interface.**

## Setting Up

Go to **Settings > White Label > Branding**.

## Fields

### Company Name

Your agency or company name. This replaces "Crawlix" in report headers, footers, and title pages.

### Logo

Upload your company logo. Requirements:

- Format: PNG (transparent background recommended)
- The image is stored as Base64 within the app settings
- Recommended size: 200×60px or similar horizontal logo dimensions

### Contact Email

Your agency's contact email address. Appears in the report footer.

### Contact Phone (optional)

Your phone number. Appears alongside the email in the footer.

### Website URL (optional)

Your agency website. Appears as a link in the report footer.

## Applying Branding

Once configured, your branding is automatically applied to all new PDF, HTML, and Excel exports. You don't need to re-enter it for each export.

## Preview

Use the **Preview** button to generate a sample report page with your branding applied before running a full export.

## Removing Your Branding

You can clear your branding settings at any time. Reports will revert to default Crawlix branding until you reconfigure.
