## Overview

By default, exported reports include a "Powered by Crawlix" attribution in the footer. Agency tier users can remove this entirely for fully white-labeled deliverables.

**Requires Agency tier.**

## How to Remove It

1. Go to **Settings > White Label > Branding**.
2. Find the **"Remove Powered by Crawlix"** toggle.
3. Enable it.
4. Save settings.

All subsequent exports will omit the Crawlix attribution.

## What This Enables

With the attribution removed and your own branding applied (company name, logo, colors, fonts), your exported reports appear as your agency's own SEO analysis tool output. Clients see your brand, not Crawlix.

## Reports Affected

The "Powered by Crawlix" removal applies to:

- PDF reports
- Interactive HTML reports
- Excel exports (footer attribution, if present)

## Re-enabling Attribution

You can re-enable the "Powered by Crawlix" attribution at any time by toggling the setting off.

## Use Cases

- **White-label SEO audits** — deliver professional reports under your agency brand
- **Client portal integration** — export reports to include in client portals or dashboards without third-party attribution
- **Reseller / sub-agency relationships** — if you manage SEO for other agencies who have their own clients
