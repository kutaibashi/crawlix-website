## Activation Fails

**Symptom:** Entering the license key returns an error or fails silently.

**Checks:**

- Ensure you have an internet connection. License validation requires an outbound HTTPS request to `api.lemonsqueezy.com`.
- Check that you're entering the complete license key — they are long alphanumeric strings. Copy-paste directly from your purchase email.
- If you're behind a corporate firewall, verify that outbound HTTPS to external domains is allowed.

## "License Already Used" or Device Limit Reached

**Symptom:** Activation fails with a message that the license is already in use.

**What's happening:** You've reached the device limit for your tier (Free: 1, Pro: 1, Agency: 5).

**Fixes:**

- On your old machine (if accessible): Go to **Settings > License > Manage Devices > Deactivate** to free up the slot.
- If you can't access the old machine: Contact **support@crawlix.app** with your license key and a brief explanation. The team can manually release the activation.

## License Shows as Expired

**Symptom:** An active license is showing as expired.

**Checks:**

- Verify your system clock is correct. Incorrect date/time on your machine can cause expiry checks to fail incorrectly.
- For annual licenses: check the expiry date in **Settings > License**. If it genuinely expired, renew at crawlix.app/pricing.

## Lost License Key

**Symptom:** You've lost the email with your license key.

**Recovery options:**

- Check your email inbox for the original purchase confirmation from Lemon Squeezy.
- Log in to your Lemon Squeezy account at [app.lemonsqueezy.com](https://app.lemonsqueezy.com) — your license key is available in your purchase history.

## Transferring to a New Machine

1. On your old machine: **Settings > License > Manage Devices > Deactivate**.
2. Install Crawlix on the new machine.
3. Go to **Settings > License > Enter License Key** and activate with the same key.
