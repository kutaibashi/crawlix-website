## Title Tags

- **Missing title tag** — page has no `<title>` element
- **Duplicate title tags** — two or more pages share the same title
- **Too short** — title under 30 characters (under-optimized)
- **Too long** — title over 60 characters (likely to be truncated in SERPs)
- **Multiple title tags** — more than one `<title>` element on the page

## Meta Descriptions

- **Missing meta description** — page has no `<meta name="description">` tag
- **Duplicate meta descriptions** — two or more pages share the same description
- **Too short** — under 50 characters
- **Too long** — over 160 characters (likely to be truncated)

## Heading Structure

- **Missing H1** — page has no `<h1>` element
- **Multiple H1s** — more than one `<h1>` on the page
- **H1 too long** — excessively long H1 text
- **Heading hierarchy issues** — H2 appearing before H1, or heading levels skipped (e.g., H1 followed immediately by H4)
- **Empty headings** — headings with no text content

## Open Graph

Open Graph tags control how your pages appear when shared on social media (Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.):

- Missing `og:title`
- Missing `og:description`
- Missing `og:image`
- Missing `og:url`
- `og:image` URL returning a 4xx/5xx error

## Twitter Cards

- Missing `twitter:card`
- Missing `twitter:title`
- Missing `twitter:description`

## Pagination

- Missing `rel="prev"` and `rel="next"` on paginated pages

## Sitemap

- Pages not listed in the XML sitemap
- `noindex` pages appearing in the sitemap (they should be excluded)
- Sitemap URLs returning non-200 status codes

## Duplicate and Thin Content

- **Duplicate page content** — near-identical pages detected via content similarity analysis
- **Thin content** — pages with very low word count (below threshold, configurable in settings)
