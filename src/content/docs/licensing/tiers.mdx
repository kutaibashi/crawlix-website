## Free Tier

The Free tier is a fully functional version of Crawlix with a 100-page crawl limit. No account required, no time limit. All core SEO checks are included.

It's a great starting point for small sites or for evaluating Crawlix before upgrading.

## Pro Tier

Pro is designed for SEO professionals, freelancers, and small agencies. Key additions over Free:

- 50,000 pages per crawl
- All Google integrations (GA4, GSC, PageSpeed Insights)
- Professional export formats (Excel, PDF, Interactive HTML)
- Scheduled automated crawls

## Agency Tier

Agency adds everything needed for running an SEO agency:

- Unlimited pages per crawl
- 5 device activations for your team
- White-label branding on all exports
- API export for custom integrations
- Sharing extraction rule templates with the community
