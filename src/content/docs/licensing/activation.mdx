## Purchasing a License

Purchase Pro or Agency licenses at [crawlix.app/pricing](/pricing) via Lemon Squeezy. After completing your purchase, you'll receive a license key by email.

## Activating Your License

1. Open Crawlix.
2. Go to **Settings > License**.
3. Click **Enter License Key**.
4. Paste your license key.
5. Click **Activate**.

An internet connection is required for the one-time activation. Crawlix validates the key with the Lemon Squeezy API.

## Hardware Fingerprinting

When you activate a license, Crawlix creates a hardware fingerprint of your machine. This fingerprint is used to:

- Associate the activation with your specific device
- Enforce the device limit for your tier (1 for Pro, 5 for Agency)

The fingerprint is a hash of hardware characteristics — no personally identifiable hardware information is stored.

## After Activation

Once activated:

- Your license status is shown in **Settings > License** (tier, expiry date, and number of activations used).
- Pro/Agency features are immediately available.
- Annual licenses remain active until the expiry date.
- Perpetual licenses are active indefinitely with no expiry.

## Reactivation

If you reinstall Crawlix on the same machine, you'll need to reactivate your license. If you've hit your device limit, see [Managing Devices](/docs/licensing/devices) for how to free up a slot.

## Offline Use

After initial activation, Crawlix works offline. The license check runs on activation, not on every launch.
