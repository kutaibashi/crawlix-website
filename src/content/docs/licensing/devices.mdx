## Device Limits

| Tier | Max Devices |
|------|-------------|
| Free | 1 |
| Pro | 1 |
| Agency | 5 |

Each license activation uses one device slot. Once all slots are used, you must deactivate a device before activating on a new one.

## Checking Your Activations

In Crawlix, go to **Settings > License > Manage Devices**. You'll see a list of all active devices for your license, including the device name and activation date.

## Deactivating a Device

If you want to activate Crawlix on a new machine:

1. On the old machine (if accessible): Go to **Settings > License > Manage Devices > Deactivate This Device**.
2. The slot is immediately freed up.
3. Activate on the new machine using the same license key.

## Transferring When You Can't Access the Old Device

If you no longer have access to the old machine (hardware failure, lost device, etc.):

1. Contact support at **support@crawlix.app**.
2. Provide your license key and a brief explanation.
3. The support team will manually release the activation slot.

Allow 1–2 business days for device reset requests.

## Agency Team Management

For Agency licenses with 5 device slots, you can distribute activations across your team. Each team member installs Crawlix on their machine and activates using the shared Agency license key.

See [Team Management](/docs/licensing/teams) for more details on Agency team workflows.
