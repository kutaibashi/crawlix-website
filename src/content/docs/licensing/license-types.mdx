## Two License Types

For both Pro and Agency tiers, you can choose between:

- **Annual license** — paid yearly, includes updates throughout the subscription period
- **Perpetual license** — one-time payment, includes 1 year of updates from the purchase date

## Annual License

**Pricing:** Pro $149/yr, Agency $399/yr

- Pay once per year to keep your license active
- Includes all software updates and new features for the duration of your subscription
- When you renew, your subscription continues seamlessly
- If you don't renew, the app stops working after the expiry date

**Best for:** Users who want to stay on the latest version with new features and bug fixes continuously.

## Perpetual License

**Pricing:** Pro $249, Agency $699

- One-time payment — no recurring charges
- Includes all software updates released within the first year of your purchase
- After the first year, the app continues to work with the version you have — it does not stop working
- To get updates after the first year: purchase a new perpetual license at a discounted upgrade price

**Best for:** Users who don't need every update, prefer predictable one-time costs, or use the app occasionally.

## Comparing the Two

| | Annual | Perpetual |
|--|--------|-----------|
| Payment | Yearly recurring | One-time |
| Updates | Continuous | 1 year from purchase |
| After expiry | App stops working | App keeps working, no new updates |
| Long-term cost | More (if used for many years) | Less (if used for many years) |

## Which Should You Choose?

- If you're actively using Crawlix for client work and want the latest features: **Annual**
- If you use Crawlix occasionally or want to avoid subscriptions: **Perpetual**
