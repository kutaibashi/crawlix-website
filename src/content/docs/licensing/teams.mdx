## Overview

The Agency license supports up to 5 device activations, making it suitable for small SEO teams. All team members can use the full feature set — including white-label exports and API access — under a single license.

**Requires Agency tier.**

## Distributing Seats to Your Team

1. Purchase an Agency license at [crawlix.app/pricing](/pricing).
2. You receive a single license key by email.
3. Share the license key with your team members.
4. Each team member installs Crawlix on their machine and activates with the shared key.
5. Up to 5 machines can be activated simultaneously.

## Checking Seat Usage

The license holder (or any team member) can view all active devices in **Settings > License > Manage Devices**.

## Removing a Team Member

If a team member leaves or no longer needs access:

1. Have them deactivate Crawlix on their machine: **Settings > License > Manage Devices > Deactivate**.
2. Their seat is released and can be used by a new team member.

If the person is no longer available to deactivate themselves, contact support@crawlix.app to release the seat.

## Shared Configurations

Saved crawl configurations are stored locally on each machine, not synced. To share a configuration:

1. Export the configuration file from your machine.
2. Send it to the team member.
3. They import it in **Settings > Saved Configurations > Import**.

## Central Billing

All 5 seats are covered by a single Agency subscription. There's no per-seat fee — the Agency price covers your entire team.

## Contact for Onboarding

For onboarding assistance or questions about team setup, contact **support@crawlix.app**.
