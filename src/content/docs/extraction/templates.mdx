## What Are Templates?

Templates are pre-built sets of extraction rules for common website types. Applying a template instantly creates multiple extraction rules tailored to that site type.

## Built-In Presets

### E-commerce

Creates rules to extract:

- Product name
- SKU / product ID
- Price
- Currency
- Stock / availability status
- Product category
- Brand

### Blog / Editorial

Creates rules to extract:

- Publish date
- Author name
- Article category / tags
- Read time (if shown on page)

### Contact Pages

Creates rules to extract:

- Phone number
- Email address
- Physical address

## Applying a Preset

1. Go to **Settings > Custom Extraction > Templates**.
2. Browse available presets.
3. Click **Apply** on the preset you want.
4. Crawlix creates the corresponding extraction rules using best-guess CSS selectors based on common patterns.
5. Review each rule — you may need to adjust the selectors to match your specific site's HTML structure.

## Customizing After Applying

After applying a preset, all rules are fully editable. You might need to:

- Update a CSS selector to match your site's specific class names
- Add transforms (e.g., `trim`, `number`) to clean up extracted values
- Add validation rules (e.g., require price on all product pages)

## Saving Your Own Templates

After configuring rules for a site type, you can save your configuration as a custom template for reuse on future projects.

1. Select the rules you want to save.
2. Click **Save as Template**.
3. Give the template a name and description.

Your saved templates appear alongside the built-in presets.
